Vegetable Soup

Just Soup

You won't meat any ingredients that aren't vegetables here.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp vegetable stock
  • 5 large tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 1 tin of tomato paste
  • 1 liter of water
  • Olive Oil
  • 2 leeks (diced)
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 red onion (diced)
  • salt and pepper
  • 4 medium potatoes (boiled, peeled, and diced)
  • 1/2 small cabbage (chopped)
  • mixed herbs (dried or fresh)

Directions

  1. Add Stock, tomatoes, and tomato paste along with 1 liter of water to large pot
  2. Stir Thoroughly
  3. In a pan, lightly fry leeks with garlic, onion, herbs, salt, and pepper until garlic and onions are evenly browned
  4. Add the boiled potatoes and cabbage, and top up water in the pot as needed. The tomato base should not be too thick or too watery at all times. Boil until cabbage is soft and tender, and remove from heat.
  5. Season with additional herbs, salt, and pepper to taste, and serve warm with fresh bread.
