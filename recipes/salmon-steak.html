Ingredients

  • 2 Thick Salmon Fillets (deboned, with skin)
  • Sea Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepper
  • 1 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 3 cloves of garlic (finely minced)
  • Fresh Dill Stalks
  • 3 tbsp sour cream

Directions

Note: At least 3 hours prior to cooking, start to marinade your salmon.

  1. Firstly, roll salmon lightly in sea salt. In a bowl, add the olive oil, pepper, teriyaki sauce, garlic, and a pinch of dill, and mix well.
  2. Use your hands to rub this mixture into the salmon, making sure both the skin and meat are evenly seasoned. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate.
  3. When the salmon has absorbed the marinade, heat a pan to maximum heat and drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  4. Cook salmon skin side down until skin is crispy, then turn over and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes.
  5. To serve, mix plucked dill leaves with sour cream and place a dollop on the side of your dish. Enjoy!
